Forschungsförderungsdifferenzen im deutschen Föderalismus

Vision

The Dataset we got was about the DFG (Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft). The organisations purpose is to support research directly with money or providing infrastructure. The data shows which projects got funded by the DFG and what kind of project it is. Our vision for this project is to bring more transperancy into funding. We wanted to show how the different federal states of germany have different focuses on project fundings. We observed that they often differentiate in their funding policy and therefore different subject area like medicine or computer science were more funded. We had to enrich the given data with external data sources because it misses some important parts.
